Dr. Ergin Er

Plastic Surgery

Clinic: Istanbul Aesthetic Center, Turkey, Istanbul
Experience: 25 years

With a background of 25 years in plastic surgery, Dr. Ergin Er. is a famous Turkish surgeon who is currently associated with Istanbul Aesthetic Center. He has extensive knowledge in performing high-quality plastic surgery techniques with accuracy and precision. He is fluent in English and Turkish. He worked as Chief Military Doctor at Turkish Navy, Istanbul He worked at Transmed Aesthetic Clinic as Plastic Surgeon. Dr. Ergin worked as a Plastic surgeon at Anatomica Hospital.
